Lead a Chapter, Make an Impact

Chapter leaders are the heart of our mission. Want to bring NextScholars to your school or community? You'll gain valuable mentorship opportunities and strong resume boosters.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Recruiting students and teachers to join your local chapter.
  • Organizing local classes, workshops, and secure meeting spaces.
  • Hosting events that promote leadership and learning among peers.
  • Building strong connections between your chapter and the larger NextScholars network.
  • Team Management (Overseeing the 10 management roles below).

Free Enrollment is Open!

Enrollment for NextScholars acceleration classes is now open! Our programs are offered for **K–5th graders** and **6–8th graders**, and they are completely **free of charge**, making it the only program of its kind available today.

  • Small Class Sizes: Each acceleration class is capped at 25 students to keep groups small and focused for individualized attention.
  • Community Focused: Students will be placed into classes by their local chapter, ensuring they learn alongside peers in their own community.
  • Holistic Learning: Classes cover core enrichment subjects, hands-on projects, and leadership-focused activities to spark curiosity and growth.

Enrollment is first come, first served, and once seats are filled, waitlists will be created.
