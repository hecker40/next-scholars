Chapter Management Structure

Each successful chapter is built around these essential, high-impact roles:

  • President/Leader (Interviewed)
  • Vice President (Interviewed)
  • Secretary (Officer Role)
  • Treasurer (Officer Role)
  • Outreach Director (Officer Role)
  • Marketing & Media Lead (Officer Role)
  • Events Coordinator (Officer Role)
  • Education/Programs Lead (Officer Role)
  • Recruitment Chair (Officer Role)
  • Tech/Logistics Lead (Officer Role)

Area Managers

Area Managers oversee multiple chapters, provide support, and ensure smooth operations across a region.
